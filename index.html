The Legend of the Forest

An unique journey in the Forest

Explore the wild life from fascinating perspectives ! You are the new chosen Guardian of a fantasy Forest. Your duty is to help the world threatened by darkness. Slip into your different animal shapes to solve the mystery behind this world.

A Shapeshifting Heroine

This game is the initiatory ritual for the heroine. She takes over the duty to preserve the balance in the wildlife. Her ability to shapeshift will help herself in solving inextricable situations.

Deer

Her fist animal shape is the deer. She is able to charge and break objects on her path.

Bear

Her second form is the bear. With this form, she may move objects from one place to another.

Wolf

Her last shape is the wolf. In this shape, she will be able to hunt and scare off preys leading to specific location.
